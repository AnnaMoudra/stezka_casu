1 SECOND

Do you know how long it really is? Too short? Or too long?

does a milisecond last longer than a wing beat of a butterfly?

find out with your own mouse ...

Nanosecond, microsecond, millisecond, picosecond, who knows? And how long do they even last and what's the point? You can find out in our timepath - a timeline that shows how long a second lasts and what all gets squeezed into it.

Use the switches in the top right corner to switch between the axes - the top one (green) shows the time from one microsecond to one second. That's how long a second would be if 1 microsecond was the "length" of 1 pixel on your screen.

The lower (pink) axis is for even smaller units - from one picosecond to one microsecond. This is how long the time span would be if 1 picosecond was "length" of 1 pixel.
